### Software

I'm **creator and lead developer** of [**LrGeniusAI**](https://lrgenius.com): a Lightroom Classic plugin that uses LLMs to tag and describe images, with semantic search and support for local (Ollama, LM Studio) and cloud (Gemini, Vertex AI) models. Under the [LrGenius](https://github.com/LrGenius) org.

I also maintain the [**LR Immich Plugin**](https://github.com/bmachek/lrc-immich-plugin), which connects Lightroom Classic with [Immich](https://immich.app/) for export, publish, and import workflows.
