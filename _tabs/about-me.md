#### Short version
I'm **Bastian Machek**, an IT nerd in his mid-forties living in southern Germany. Married father of a little boy.
I'm retired due to a rare neurological disease called [CIDP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chronic_inflammatory_demyelinating_polyneuropathy), so I focus on what I love: photography and building free software.

I'm **creator and lead developer** of [**LrGeniusAI**](https://lrgenius.com) (under the [LrGenius](https://github.com/LrGenius) org): a Lightroom Classic plugin that uses LLMs to tag and describe images, with semantic search and support for local (Ollama, LM Studio) and cloud (Gemini, Vertex AI) models. I also maintain other Lightroom plugins and tools.
